Sponsor Us

A robotics team is expensive to run, and we need your help! From competition fees and travel expenses to materials and tools, building a competitive robot and fostering a successful team takes a lot of resources. Your support ensures we can continue to grow, innovate, and represent our community.

Why Sponsor Us?

  • Support Youth and STEM: Help inspire the next generation of innovators, engineers, and leaders.
  • Visibility for Your Business: Gain exposure through logo placements on our robot, team uniforms, and event banners, as well as mentions on our website and social media.
  • Community Impact: Show your commitment to education, teamwork, and the local community by supporting a program that changes lives.

Our Current Sponsors

We are incredibly grateful to our current sponsors who make it possible for us to compete and innovate. Thank you for believing in our mission and helping us bring our visions to life!
