Our Team

Who We Are

Hello! We are Robeartics 19500, a dedicated robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. Our mascot, Bearing Jr., represents the spirit of innovation, strength, and perseverance that defines us.

Mission Statement

At Robeartics, our mission is to inspire creativity, foster teamwork, and promote STEM education through competitive robotics.

Values and Culture

We pride ourselves on being a team that values respect, inclusivity, hard work, and a passion for learning. Every member of Robeartics is encouraged to contribute their unique skills and ideas, making us stronger together.
