Outreach

Robeartics is dedicated to making a positive impact in our community through outreach initiatives.

We engage in various activities, from robotics workshops and demonstrations to community service projects, aiming to inspire the next generation of innovators and leaders. Our outreach efforts focus on inclusivity, education, and making STEM accessible to all.

Stay tuned for updates and stories about our outreach events and how we're making a difference!

WCP

The World Connections Program (WCP) connects FTC teams globally, promoting collaboration, cultural exchange, and the sharing of robotics strategies. By engaging with government officials and educators, WCP raises awareness of FIRST programs and their impact on STEM education. Initiatives like discussions with the Chief Education Officer of Cyprus aim to expand FTC opportunities worldwide, empowering teams to create a network of support, innovation, and shared learning in the spirit of FIRST.

STEM 4 THEM

Our fall scrimmage welcomed TDSB elementary students and their parents for a day of learning and fun. Focused on FIRST LEGO League (FLL) and the theme "Depths of Discoveries," the event introduced marine life, ocean conservation, and anti-littering through interactive activities and games. We successfully taught FLL fundamentals to around 60 students, fostering curiosity, teamwork, and environmental awareness in an engaging and memorable way.
