Press It

We believe that creativity and compassion should be accessible to everyone. That’s why we’re dedicated to providing high-quality, custom-made products at prices that won’t break the bank. Whether you’re personalizing an item for yourself, celebrating a special occasion, or promoting your brand, we make it easy and affordable to bring your vision to life. When you choose PressIt, you’re not just creating something unique—you’re making a difference in the lives of children and their families.

Mission Statement

At PressIt, giving back is at the heart of what we do. We’re proud to support Holland Bloorview Kids Rehabilitation Hospital, a remarkable organization dedicated to empowering children with disabilities through cutting-edge robotics and innovative therapies. By donating 20% of our profits, we’re helping to advance their vital work and positively impact the lives of countless children.

Our Services

At PressIt, we offer a range of customizable products and services tailored to your needs. Our offerings include T-Shirts, Hoodies, Zip-up Sweaters, Tote Bags, and Heat Press Renting. Each item can be personalized to match your unique style or brand.
