WHO ARE WE?

❮ ❯

Hello, we are team Robeartics #19500, a FIRST robotics team consisting of 15 passionate members from grades 10 to 12. Together, we strive to create opportunities for people to explore technology, develop their skills, and inspire gracious professionalism. Beyond our competitions, we actively spread the word of STEM and empower others to see the unwavering potential in robotics.
